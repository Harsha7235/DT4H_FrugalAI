🏥 Twin Vital Pulse – Autonomous Healthcare Digital Twin Platform

Live Application

Twin Vital Pulse is an AI-powered Healthcare Digital Twin platform designed to create virtual patient replicas for predictive healthcare, disease risk forecasting, organ stress monitoring, treatment simulation, and explainable clinical decision support.

Live Demo:

https://twin-vital-pulse.base44.app

Overview

Twin Vital Pulse combines Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Explainable AI, Digital Twin technology, and Healthcare Analytics into a unified platform capable of monitoring patient health, forecasting disease progression, simulating medication effects, and supporting evidence-based clinical decisions.

The platform creates a dynamic virtual representation of a patient using clinical biomarkers, physiological parameters, disease conditions, medication history, and longitudinal health records.

Unlike traditional healthcare dashboards that provide static reports, Twin Vital Pulse continuously analyzes patient data and simulates future outcomes through a digital twin framework.

Problem Statement

Non-communicable diseases (NCDs) such as:

Type 2 Diabetes
Coronary Artery Disease (CAD)
Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)
Hypertension
Heart Failure
account for the majority of global healthcare burden.

Traditional healthcare systems often identify deterioration after symptoms become severe.

Twin Vital Pulse aims to shift healthcare from reactive treatment to proactive prevention through AI-driven simulation and predictive analytics.
